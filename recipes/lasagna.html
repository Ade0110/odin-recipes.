Lasagna recipe

Description

A classic recipe of one of the most loved foods in the world. The ultimate comfort food = homemade lasagna. Family and friends gather around the dinner table at any time for this lasagna recipe.

    Ingredients
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
    Steps
  1. Heat the olive oil
  2. Cook the meat, onions and tomatoes
  3. Layer the cooked meat, onions and tomatoes with cheese
  4. Cook for 45 minutes
